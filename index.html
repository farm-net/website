Transforming India's fresh produce game

Our Mission

Welcome to Farmnet, where we're reshaping India's fresh produce landscape. With 150 million farmers, a complex supply chain, and 13 million small grocery retailers, efficiency has been a challenge. We're here to change that.


We start by assisting small grocery store owners in easy produce procurement, saving them 3-4 hours daily. Fresh produce is delivered before 7 am, ensuring store owners sell the freshest goods to early-morning customers. 


Join us at Farmnet as we revolutionize the fresh produce industry, one small grocery store at a time.
